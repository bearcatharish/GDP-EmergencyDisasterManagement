How to add a new volunteer?
Go to Volunteers page and click on “Create New Volunteer” button. Then fill all the details of the volunteer like name, age, address etc and finally click on “add” button. Then the volunteer will be added in the Volunteers page.
How to create a group?
Go to Volunteers page and click on “Create Group” button. A pop up will be displayed and enter the group id, name, description and group lead Mail Id and finally click on “Add Group” button.
How should I prepare in the event of an emergency?
Emergency preparedness is also an individual responsibility. Team strongly people to familiarize themselves with the plans and precautions currently in place at the university, but also to become aware of the measures they need to take to protect themselves and others in an emergency situation.
Does the Response team have a disaster plan?
The University is required by state and federal regulations to have a response and recovery plan. This plan covers everything from earthquakes, tsunamis, fires to floods. This plan is maintained by the Emergency Management Team and is continuously updated. The current version is available on this Web site.
What if there is an active shooter?
If you are directly involved in an active shooter situation-focus on your own survival, be alert, try to figure out what’s happening and decide your best course of action
Can a volunteer work simultaneously in two operations?
No. Each volunteer can work only at a single prescribed area
